At Ubunifu , we nurture diverse aspirations, teaching coding through startup building to create versatile tech problem solvers.

01

Admissions Process

Personalized Onboarding: We begin with a personalized interview to understand each student’s goals, whether they want to learn coding, develop a project, or start a tech company. This tailored approach ensures that we meet diverse needs right from the start.

02

Learning Methodology

Project-Based Learning: Our curriculum emphasizes hands-on experience, where students learn coding and development by working on real-world projects. This method allows students to apply theoretical knowledge practically, enhancing their problem-solving skills.

03

Dynamic Class Structure

Flexible Class Hours: Classes run for 4 hours a day, providing a balanced mix of instruction and independent project work. We also offer short virtual courses to cater to different learning paces and styles, making education accessible for everyone.

04

Success Measurement

Startup Launch and Feedback: Success is measured by students' ability to build and launch a market-ready startup by graduation. Regular pitching sessions and feedback from industry experts help students refine their products and business models, ensuring they are equipped for the tech landscape.
